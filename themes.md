# Habit Tracker App - Themes and Design Guidelines

## 1. Overall Aesthetic
- **Inspiration:** Notion-style.
- **Core Philosophy:** Minimalist, highly breathable, deeply focused on productivity, and impactful without generic beautifications. Highly quality-focused UX.

## 2. Typography
- **Primary Font:** `Inter` throughout the UI.
- **Quote Font:** A beautiful Calligraphy-like font used exclusively for daily quotes on the homescreen.
- **Hierarchy:** Rely on typography weights (Bold, SemiBold, Regular) and styles (Italics, Uppercase) to establish proper visual hierarchy, rather than overwhelming colors. 

## 3. Color Management
- **Default Baseline:** Strict Black and White.
- **Text & Accents:** Black or dark colors for primary information; varying shades of Grey for secondary, less important text.
- **Functional Colors:** Colors are reserved strictly for user-created entities (e.g., User Habits, To-dos) so that user content "pops" against the minimalist layout. 

## 4. Layout and Space
- **Whitespace:** Emphasize generous margins and paddings. The app must feel open and structured.
- **Elevations:** Use subtle shadowing and elevations intentionally (e.g., elevating the background for the daily quote) to distinguish layers without clutter.

## 5. Animations
- **Style:** Extremely subtle, crisp, and smooth.
- **Rule:** Implement the latest Android animation standards (Jetpack Compose). Avoid any chaotic, bouncy, or unnecessarily long animations that distract from productivity.
